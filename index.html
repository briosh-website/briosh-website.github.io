Small site which I manage in my spare time, which was born from my desire to tidy up a bit some material and some thoughts.

With this in mind, I decided to divide the site into different sections. The Home, to which you are directed every time you try to access the site. About, which is this page, briefly discusses the site, dealing with both the motivational and structural parts. Photography is dedicated to images and photos, something that I've been very passionate about lately. Food is a section that I'm not yet sure about how to structure, but ideally it should talk about food. In Math I plan to put some useful material regarding the specific branch I deal with when I work, namely post-quantum cryptography, with particular reference to digital signatures based on linear codes. Then there are Mountains and Music, for which I have a lot of ideas in mind but I would like to be more convinced on how to develop them. Finally, Miscellanea encloses all the remaining things.
